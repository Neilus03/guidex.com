GUIDEX

Guided Synthetic Data Generation for Zero-Shot Information Extraction

Abstract

Information Extraction systems usually break when label definitions change. GUIDEX removes that bottleneck: it automatically infers a domain-specific schema, writes detailed guidelines, and generates synthetic, schema-compliant examples. Fine-tuning LLaMa 3.1 with those examples raises the state of the art on seven zero-shot NER benchmarks, gaining up to +7 F1 without human labels and almost +3 F1 when combined with gold data. Code, models and the dataset will be released.
